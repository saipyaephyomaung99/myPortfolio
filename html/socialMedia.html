Social Media Project

I made this porject when I was selected as OJT student in CODELAB A-Z training.

For frontend, I used BootstrapV . And for backend, I used Laravel for this project.

Users can

If you've already had an account, you can login into your account. If you don't you have to register new account to use this website.

After you logged in, feed will show posts of users that you've followed. You can like or comment on their posts.

You can also search users you may know and follow/unfollow their accounts.

You can create new post. It will show on your profile and feeds of users that followed your account.

After you posted, you can still edit or delete your post and delete every comments in your post. But you can delete only your comment in other users' posts.

If other users followed your account or interacted your posts, you will get notifications.

Account Settings

You can edit your profile by changing infos about you.

If you think your account password is not strong or secured enough, you can change your account passoword.

If you don't want to exist your account, you can delete your account. And your account can't be found anymore by other users.
