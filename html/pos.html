SPPM
POS system

Authentication

If your account role is an admin, you will get to admin side when you login.

If your account role is a user, you will get to user home page when you login.

Category List (Admin Side)

As an admin, you can search existing categories and also you can create new category for your website.

As an admin, you can also edit your category update or delete existing category on your website.

Category List (User Side)

As a user, you can choose to view categories as you like or sort to view all products ascending or descending on the website.

Products List (Admin Side)

As an admin, you can add new products available on your website with necessary informations.

As an admin, you can serach what products are existing, view every products info and remove existing products on your website.

Order List (User Side)

As a user, to make an order , you can add every products as many as you want into the cart.

After you added products as you like into the cart, you can still adjust the quantity of products in the cart. Aslo the price will be adjusted. After that, you can clear cart or proceed to checkout.

After checkout your order, you can still watch your order history if it's pending,accepted or cancelled.

Order List (Admin Side)

After user made an order, it comes to admin side along with order code. As an admin, you can check orders thru order codes and make changes if their orders are accepted or rejected.

Contact form

As a user, you can contact to admin team about bugs, or for advices or suggestions.

As an admin, you can view users' messages. And also you can delete or reply if it's necessary.

Admin and User List (Admin only)

As an admin, you can see who are on admin team and change their account roles from admin to user.

As an admin, you can also see users list on you website and also you can promote their account roles to admin.

Account Settings

As an admin, you can edit your account info and change your account password.

Like an admin, users can also edit account info and change their account passwords.
